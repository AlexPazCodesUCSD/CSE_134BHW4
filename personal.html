Teaching

One of my recent career ideas is becoming a CS teacher for K-12. I would love to teach the youth Computer Science the same way my CS teachers taught me, which led me to where I am today. In my pursuits I have taken three courses on CS teaching (CSE 198, EDS 124AR, EDS 124BR).

In CSE 198, I got to make a lesson plan introducing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for K-12 students. I even got to teach K-12 students my lesson plan, which was amazing to see my work get to the students.
